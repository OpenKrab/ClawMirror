# ClawMirror

ClawMirror is an OpenClaw skill that creates beautiful, structured daily notes of your conversations with Claw and syncs them automatically to your favorite PKM (Personal Knowledge Management) tools like Obsidian and Logseq.

## Features

- End-of-session auto sync or manual `/mirror` trigger.
- Customizable templates for Obsidian (Daily Notes) and Logseq (Journals).
- Zero-cloud, local-first operation.

## Setup

Edit `config.yaml` to point to your PKM vaults and run `python src/main.py`.
